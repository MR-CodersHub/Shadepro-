General FAQ

Got questions about film curing, protection specs, or mobile detailing? We've got answers.

Window Tinting
How long does it take for window tint to cure? +
Curing typically takes between 2 to 5 days depending on the weather conditions and humidity in Florida. During this period, you may notice small water bubbles or a hazy appearance. This is completely normal and will evaporate naturally. Do not roll down your windows for at least 3 days.
What is the difference between carbon and ceramic tint? +
Carbon tint uses carbon particles to absorb infrared light, blocking up to 40% of solar heat. Ceramic tint is infused with advanced nano-ceramic particles that filter out infrared heat, rejecting up to 60-65% of solar heat. Ceramic tint provides superior clarity and heat rejection.
Is your window film signal-friendly? +
Yes, all our carbon and ceramic films are 100% non-metallic. They will not interfere with cellular reception, GPS navigation, satellite radio, Bluetooth, or tire pressure monitors.
Paint Protection Film (PPF)
How does PPF self-healing work? +
Our premium clear PPF is constructed from elastomeric polyurethane polymers. When exposed to heat (like direct sunlight or warm water), the polymers relax and return to their original, flat configuration, making light scratches and swirl marks disappear.
Will Paint Protection Film turn yellow over time? +
No, we install only high-grade XPEL and STEK films featuring premium acrylic adhesives. They include integrated UV inhibitors that prevent yellowing, cracking, or lifting, and are backed by a 10-year manufacturer warranty.
Mobile Service
What are the requirements for a mobile installation? +
For premium window tinting and PPF installations, we require a clean, enclosed garage or shaded carport shield protected from strong winds and airborne dust. We also need access to a standard electrical outlet. If you don't have a garage, contact us, and we can discuss alternative arrangements.
Do you charge extra for mobile service? +
No, our mobile service is included in all standard packages within our designated service area (Miami & surrounding cities). There are no hidden call-out fees.
