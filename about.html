Our Story

Driven by Perfection

Founded in 2013, ShadePro started with a simple vision: to deliver premium window tinting and detailing services without the inconvenience of visiting a physical shop. We built a custom mobile system that replicates a dust-free workshop environment inside a state-of-the-art van.

Today, we protect and enhance hundreds of luxury vehicles, supercars, SUVs, and daily drivers every single month. We use only premium ceramic films and self-healing clear films, backed by our lifetime warranty.
