Service Information

What You Need To Know

Transparent policies, UV benefits, warranty details, and everything you need before booking your service.

UV Protection Benefits

Our films block 99% of harmful UV rays, protecting your skin from prolonged exposure and preventing interior fading, leather cracking, and dashboard discoloration.

Warranty Information

Window tinting films carry a Lifetime Warranty against bubbling, peeling, and fading. PPF packages include a 10-Year Manufacturer Warranty covering yellowing and adhesion failure.

Service Duration

Standard window tinting takes 2–3 hours. PPF installations typically require 1–2 days for complete cure. Combo packages may require scheduling over two appointments.

Mobile Service Charges

We offer complimentary mobile service within 35 miles of Miami. No hidden travel fees. Service to extended areas incurs a flat $45–$85 travel charge depending on distance.

Add-On Services

Upgrade any package with: Sunroof tinting (+$80), Windshield strip (+$60), Headlight tinting (+$120), and Glass hydrophobic coating (+$95).

Maintenance Tips

Wait 72 hours before rolling down windows after tint installation. Always use ammonia-free cleaning products. For PPF, avoid pressure washing edges within 48 hours of installation.
